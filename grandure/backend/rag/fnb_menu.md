## Outlets

### Grandure Restaurant
Type: Fine dining
Floor: Ground floor
Hours: Breakfast 07:00-11:00 | Lunch 12:00-15:00 | Dinner 18:30-22:30
Dress code: Smart casual
Reservations: Recommended for dinner
Covers: 60
Description: Signature restaurant with modern European cuisine and locally sourced ingredients. Chef arranges personalised desserts for celebrations.

### Pool Bar and Grill
Type: Casual all-day dining
Floor: Level 1 poolside
Hours: 10:00-22:00
Dress code: Casual
Reservations: Walk-in welcome
Covers: 40
Description: Light bites, grills, cocktails and cold beverages served poolside.

### Rooftop Lounge
Type: Bar and lounge
Floor: Floor 5
Hours: 17:00-01:00
Dress code: Smart casual
Reservations: Recommended on weekends
Covers: 35
Description: Panoramic views, craft cocktails, champagne and light sharing plates. Arrive before sunset for best views.

### Spa Lounge
Type: Wellness cafe
Floor: Floor 2 (Spa level)
Hours: 09:00-20:00
Dress code: Robe or casual
Reservations: Not required
Covers: 20
Description: Wellness drinks, detox juices, herbal teas and light healthy snacks. Complimentary wellness drink for spa guests on treatment day.

### Room Service
Type: In-room dining
Hours: 06:00-23:00 (limited menu 23:00-06:00)
Surcharge: £5 per order
Description: Full menu delivered to the room.

## Occasion to Dining Guide

Anniversary or romantic → Rooftop Lounge (Whispering Angel Rose £65, panoramic views)
Birthday celebration → Grandure Restaurant (dinner, chef arranges celebration dessert)
Tired or relaxing → Room Service (Ribeye Steak £45 or Club Sandwich £18)
Poolside or daytime → Pool Bar and Grill (Aperol Spritz £14, Halloumi Fries £11)
After spa treatment → Spa Lounge (Collagen Beauty Drink £12, Turmeric Golden Latte £7)
Evening drinks → Rooftop Lounge (Espresso Martini £14, Negroni £14, arrive before sunset)
